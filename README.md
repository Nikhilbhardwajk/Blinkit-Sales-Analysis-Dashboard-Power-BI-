# Blinkit Sales Analysis Dashboard (Power BI)

## Project Overview

This project presents a comprehensive sales analysis dashboard for Blinkit – India’s Last Minute App, built using Power BI.

The objective of this analysis is to evaluate sales performance, customer satisfaction, and inventory distribution to uncover meaningful business insights and identify opportunities for optimization.

The dashboard provides interactive visualizations and KPI tracking to help stakeholders make data-driven decisions.

---

## Business Requirement

To conduct a detailed analysis of Blinkit's operational and sales data focusing on:

* Sales performance
* Customer ratings
* Inventory distribution
* Outlet performance

This enables better strategic planning, product positioning, and outlet management. 

---

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

The dashboard tracks the following core KPIs:

* Total Sales → Overall revenue generated from all items sold
* Average Sales → Average revenue per transaction
* Number of Items → Total count of different items sold
* Average Rating → Customer satisfaction indicator

These KPIs provide a quick snapshot of business health and performance. 

---

## Analysis and Insights Covered

### Sales by Fat Content

* Understand how Low Fat vs Regular items impact revenue
* Compare performance across KPIs like ratings and item count

### Sales by Item Type

* Identify top-performing product categories
* Evaluate product demand trends

### Fat Content by Outlet

* Compare outlet-wise contribution of different product types
* Helps optimize product assortment

### Sales by Outlet Establishment Year

* Analyze how outlet age affects revenue performance
* Identify maturity vs growth outlets

### Sales Distribution by Outlet Size

* Study correlation between Small / Medium / High outlet size and sales contribution

### Sales by Outlet Location Tier

* Evaluate geographic sales distribution
* Compare Tier 1, Tier 2, Tier 3 performance

### KPI Performance by Outlet Type

* Compare supermarkets and grocery stores across

  * Total Sales
  * Average Sales
  * Ratings
  * Item Visibility

All insights help improve inventory planning, outlet expansion strategy, and product focus.

## Dashboard Features

### KPI Metric Selection Slicer

The dashboard includes a KPI metric selection slicer that allows users to dynamically switch between key performance indicators such as Total Sales, Average Sales, Number of Items Sold, and Average Customer Rating. This feature enables users to analyze business performance from multiple perspectives without navigating to different reports or pages.
![image alt](https://github.com/Nikhilbhardwajk/Blinkit-Sales-Analysis-Dashboard-Power-BI-/blob/main/Dahboard.png)

---

### Outlet Location Type Filter

The dashboard includes an Outlet Location Type filter that enables users to analyze sales performance across different geographic tiers such as Tier 1, Tier 2, and Tier 3 locations. This filter helps stakeholders understand how regional market characteristics influence total sales, product demand, and customer engagement.
![image alt](https://github.com/Nikhilbhardwajk/Blinkit-Sales-Analysis-Dashboard-Power-BI-/blob/main/outlet%20location.png)

---

### Outlet Size Filter

The Outlet Size filter allows users to examine how sales performance differs across Small, Medium, and High-size outlets. This helps identify which outlet formats generate higher revenue and manage larger product assortments.
![image alt](https://github.com/Nikhilbhardwajk/Blinkit-Sales-Analysis-Dashboard-Power-BI-/blob/main/outlet%20size.png)

---

### Item Type Filter

The Item Type filter enables detailed product-level analysis by allowing users to focus on specific categories such as dairy, snacks, beverages, frozen foods, and household items. This functionality supports the identification of high-demand product segments and underperforming categories.
![image alt](https://github.com/Nikhilbhardwajk/Blinkit-Sales-Analysis-Dashboard-Power-BI-/blob/main/item%20tpye.png)

---

### Combined Filter Interactivity

The dashboard supports cross-filtering across all slicers, allowing users to perform multi-dimensional analysis such as evaluating sales of specific item categories within a particular outlet size and location tier. This interactive capability enhances analytical flexibility and enables deeper insight generation for informed decision-making.
